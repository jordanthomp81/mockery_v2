Effortless Wireframes

An online design tool made with you in mind. No more clunky, complicated wireframe tools. The simplicity of Mockery makes designing wireframes easy.

3 Core Benefits

Why Use Mockery?

User-Friendly Interface

Utilizing an intuitive layout and simple color scheme, you can easily begin building wireframes online from the first login. Unlike many of the other prototype tools, users can navigate Mockery’s simple interface without training.

Completely Customizable

Whether you are building a wireframe for an app, landing page, or website, Mockery can create any design you need. Each setting can be customized to best fit the project you are working on. There is no limit to the types of wireframes you can create.

Collaboration Made Simple

Share your latest creation with others directly from the dashboard. Mockery creates a shareable URL of your wireframe to easily send to members of your team for review. Post the link to social media and create a focus group for your design.

FEATURE 1

Wireframe Templates For Quick Designs

Mockery has a library of wireframe templates for you to choose from to start your design. Drag and drop design elements allow you to easily spin up new wireframes in minutes. Your wireframe can remain agile with the ability to quickly make changes to mockups.

Manage Projects With Your Team From One Dashboard

Collaborate on your project and share assets between the members of your team from one dashboard. Mockery boasts an easy admin interface that allows you to share individual projects between designers, developers, and clients. Uploading all assets to a shared folder gives everyone access at once.
